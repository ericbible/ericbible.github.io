Hello World!

This is the Home landing page for the various HTML, CSS and (in the future) JavaScript projects that the self-taught full stack developer journey encourages. Not only will Codecademy projects live here, this will also be a repository for my own peronal site/app ideas. If you've stumbled on this page you likely follow me on social media already, but I've put my links at the bottom just in case you've found me by "happenstance"! I started an education in technology when I graduated high-school in 2006 but after taking a job that promised fast advancement and decent money I put my education on hold. After 10 years in the Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) world, I'm rekindling my passion in technology both as a hobby and a career. Feel free to browse the sites I've played with, most are pet projects from Codecademy's Web Development path, but this current version of the repository has a custom HTML/Pure CSS drop down nav I built along with a personal Resume page ("About Me"). I would love to hear your feedback, feel free to reach out to me with comments!
